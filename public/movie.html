The best movies according to Yasir

My top 3 movies off all time

Kingdom of Heaven

A wonderful historical movie that beautifully captures the medieval crusades and stories of courage, faith, and self-discovery. If you love epic scenes with a blend of history, epic battles, and human values, then this movie is definitely for you.

Captain Jack Sparrow

Captain Jack Sparrow brilliantly brings the "Pirates of the Caribbean" series to life with his wit, humor, and reckless nature in this movie.

Train to Busan

The scariest Korean horror movie I've ever seen
